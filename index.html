The Idea

Every morning, receive something to draw. Spend one minute drawing it. Record it, post it, get on with your day.

The goal isn't perfection — it's showing up every single day.

Your Daily Routine
Sit down › Record › Draw › Post video › Post image
  • 1
    The motive Receive the item to draw — randomly generated and waiting for you before you wake up.
  • 2
    Record Set up your phone — prop it on a book, a mug, anything. No fancy setup needed.
  • 3
    Draw 😊 One minute. Quick, raw, real. No perfection — simply enjoy the doodling.
  • 4
    Post video Upload to YouTube Shorts & TikTok.
  • 5
    Post image Photo the drawing and post to Cara, Pinterest & Twitter.

⏱ Total time once accounts are set up: under 5 minutes.
